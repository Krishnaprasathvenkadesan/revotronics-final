About Us

About REC

Rajalakshmi Engineering College, started in 1997, is an autonomous institution linked to Anna University. With 26 years of academic success, it's ranked the 3rd most preferred private engineering college in Tamil Nadu. The college offers 19 UG and 11 PG courses, plus programs in management, architecture, and nursing, producing skilled professionals every year.

About ECE

The Electronics and Communication Engineering Department started in 1997. It offers courses for UG, PG, and Ph.D. students. From the beginning, the department has focused on research, helping students become skilled researchers. Each year, over 95% of eligible students get placed in top companies. The department has been producing skilled engineers who can compete globally since it began.

Event Rules

  1. All participants must register before the event deadline.
  2. Participants are required to bring their own laptops and necessary equipment.
  3. Any form of plagiarism or cheating will result in immediate disqualification.
  4. Respect the time limits set for each event and challenge.
  5. Maintain a professional and respectful demeanor towards all participants and organizers.
  6. Follow all safety guidelines and protocols set by the event organizers.
  7. The judges' decision is final and binding in all event-related matters.
  8. Have fun and embrace the spirit of learning and collaboration!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Revotronics?

Revotronics is an annual tech festival that brings together innovators, developers, and tech enthusiasts for a series of exciting events, workshops, and competitions.

How can I register for the events?

You can register for the events by visiting our official website and clicking on the 'Register' button under the respective event details.

Are there any prerequisites for participating?

Most events have no prerequisites, but some advanced workshops may require basic knowledge of the subject. Check the event details for more information.

Can I participate in multiple events?

Yes, you can participate in multiple events as long as the event timings do not overlap.
